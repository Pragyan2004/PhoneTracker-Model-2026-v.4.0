Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 2026

01 Information Collection

At PhoneTracker, we collect minimal operational data to provide our geospatial services:

  • Account Protocol: Username, email, and cryptographically hashed credentials.
  • Intercept History: Global identifiers, resolved coordinates, and chrono-timestamps.
  • Metadata: Technical headers and routing information for security synchronization.
02 Data Utilization

Your intelligence data is utilized exclusively for:

  • Maintaining your personal archive and intercept history.
  • Optimizing the accuracy of our triangulation algorithms.
  • Hardening the platform against unauthorized access or misuse.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We only share data with third-party service providers (like OpenCage) for the sole purpose of fulfillment of the tracking request, and with law enforcement if required by valid legal process.

4. Security

We implement industry-standard encryption for both data in transit and data at rest. Your search history is uniquely tied to your encrypted user ID.

Support Uplink

For data deletion requests or protocol inquiries, contact our legal division at privacy@geospatial-labs.io
